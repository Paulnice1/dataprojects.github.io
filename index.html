Retail Company Sales Data Analysis using Power BI

It was a unique project because during my data quality check on its sales data, spanning over 10,000 records, I identified significant discrepancies between some reported sales figures from different sales teams that did not align with overall market trends. The retail company needed a clear and concise way to visualise its sales performance across diverse categories, regions, and financial years to identify key trends and support strategic decision-making. Its existing reporting methods were cumbersome, relied on multiple spreadsheets, and lacked the interactive capabilities to analyze data effectively. My in-depth analysis and visualisation, leveraging Excel and Power BI improved the efficiency of the sales performance analysis significantly and enhanced decision-making, which led to a 20% increase in stakeholders' satisfaction. Our recommendation also improved the accuracy and reliability of its sales data.
