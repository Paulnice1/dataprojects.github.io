Pamon Stores Sales Analysis

It was a unique project because, during our data quality check on its sales data, spanning over 10,000 records, we identified significant discrepancies between some reported sales figures from different sales teams that did not align with overall market trends. Clients from Pamon Stores approached us because it needed a clear and concise way to visualise its sales performance across diverse categories, regions, and financial years to identify key trends and support strategic decision-making. Its existing reporting methods were cumbersome, relied on multiple spreadsheets, and lacked the interactive capabilities to analyze data effectively. Our in-depth analysis and visualisation, leveraging Excel and Power BI, improved the efficiency of the sales performance analysis significantly and enhanced decision-making, which led to a 20% increase in stakeholders' satisfaction. Our recommendation also improved the accuracy and reliability of its sales data.
